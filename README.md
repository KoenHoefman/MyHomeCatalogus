# MyHomeCatalogus

MyHomeCatalogus is a home inventory app.

---

## Core Features

- Add products to your inventory, keep track of what's in stock and manage your shopping lists.
- Manage product lists per store to keep track of where you bought your products and add them to your shopping list in the store app.
- Manage all storage locations in your home, such as the pantry, fridge, freezer, liquor cabinet...

---

## Upcoming Features

- **Recipes:** 
  - Detailed step-by-step cooking explanations.
  - Automatic stock checking: See if ingredients are in stock and add missing items directly to a shopping list.
- **Books:** Create an inventory of your book collection.
- **Music:** Create an inventory of your music collection (e.g., vinyl, CDs).
